ABOUT US

COMSAIL is jointly promoted by two professionals with vast experience in Freight Forwarding, Liner Agency and Customs Clearance. We operate for the total satisfaction of our customers.

The team is headed by technocrats, each of whom has over two decades of experience in Freight Forwarding, Liner Trade and NVOCC operations. The support team have been hand-picked from the Industry. Our experienced staff with strategic partners makes us suited to act as a one stop logistics service provider. We are an integral part of our client’s export documents, offering custom tailored ocean/sea freight logistics solutions for a wide variety of cargo. We have the necessary expertise required for handling all types of imports and exports consignments by Air or Sea. We have well established and efficient agents working as our counterparts all over the world for handling the cargo and receipt and delivery activities in their respective countries.

With reliability and Timely services, the company has grown progressively to becoming a major & reliable player in the sector. It’s currently serving clients in all continents of the world offering tailor-made services suitable for individual needs that start from the point of origin through various custom procedures to the client’s doorstep and ensures that client get personalized attention.
